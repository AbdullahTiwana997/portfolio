About Me

I’m a 21-year-old student and an enthusiastic photographer who began capturing the world through my lens at the age of 18. Living with ADHD hasn’t been a limitation — it’s become a unique perspective that shapes how I experience life. It allows me to see details, feel emotions, and express creativity in ways that deeply influence every photo I take.

I’m not just someone who clicks pictures — I tell stories, evoke moods, and chase light. Photography isn't just a hobby for me; it's the way I turn my ideas and visions into something real, something that exists beyond thought.
